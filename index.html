About Us

Aldebasy Logistics Company was established with over 35 years of experience in the field of customs clearance, transportation, and logistics services within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We are committed to providing comprehensive and reliable logistics solutions, covering customs clearance, freight forwardibuttonng, transportation, and storage, all handled with professionalism and efficiency. Our mission is to facilitate trade movement and deliver fast, high-quality services that exceed our clients’ expectations.

Our Services

Container Transport

Transportation is one of the most important logistics services. We provide import and export transport services through a modern fleet of trucks to deliver our clients’ goods safely to their warehouses. You can also track your shipment throughout the delivery process.

Customs Clearance

We provide customs clearance services for all containers through an advanced electronic system, managed by a highly qualified and experienced team. All procedures are completed within 24 hours, and customs consultancy services are also available for clients.

Saber Certification

The Saber platform is an electronic system that helps regulate and monitor markets in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It facilitates the registration of imported products, testing, inspection, and verification to ensure product quality and compliance with Saudi manufacturing standards

Storage Services

Storage services are no less important than transportation. We provide well-equipped warehouses for both import and export storage, managed by a highly skilled technical team specialized in loading and unloading operations.
